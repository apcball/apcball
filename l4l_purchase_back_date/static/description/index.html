Purchase Back Date

This Module Simplifies The Task of Backdating Transactions in Purchase Order by Allowing Users to Adjust Order Dates During Confirmation. This Helps Ensure that Associated Documents, like Receipt Schedules and Accounting Entries, Correctly Reflect the Selected Date, Ultimately Making Transaction Timelines more Efficient in Odoo.



Features

Confirm Purchase Order with a Backdate.

All Related Document such as Stock Picking, Stock Move, Move Line, Journal Entry are Reflect the Selected Date.

Multi Company Support.

No More Configuration.
